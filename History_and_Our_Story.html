Eloheh Healthcare

Your Partner in Holistic Wellness

Eloheh Healthcare was founded in 2015 with the vision of transforming healthcare into a more inclusive, compassionate, and accessible system for all. What began as a single community clinic in Portland has grown into a multi-state network focused on bridging health gaps for underserved populations.

Our early mission was simple but powerful: to remove barriers that prevent people from receiving care, whether those barriers were financial, cultural, or geographic.
