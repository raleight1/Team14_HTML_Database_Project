Our Story & History

Eloheh (pronounced *el-oh-HAY*) was founded in 2015 with a mission to provide holistic and inclusive healthcare solutions. Our journey began with a simple idea: to empower people through accessible, high-quality healthcare products and services.

We started as a small community clinic and have since grown into a national provider serving thousands across the country.

Our team is committed to ethical sourcing, sustainability, and cutting-edge care practices.

Here are some milestones in our history:

  1. 2015 - Company founded in Portland, OR
  2. 2017 - Launched our first mobile health unit
  3. 2020 - Introduced telemedicine services
  4. 2023 - Partnered with local schools and nonprofits

Our core values include:

  • Empathy
  • Innovation
  • Accessibility
  • Transparency
